The cost dimensions of agentic workloads on Amazon Web Services (AWS) are invisible to many organizations beginning their agentic journey. It's straightforward to track tokens consumed because this dimension translates directly to your bill. But most organizations can't determine the cost per request or the cost to answer a single user question.
